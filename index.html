Our Story

Proudly Serving Richmond & Steveston Since 1994

Super Grocer Pharmacy has been a trusted destination for prescription services, health advice, and personalized pharmacy care for over 30 years. Generations of families have relied on us for their everyday health needs, and we are proud to have built long-standing relationships within the community.

Our Commitment to Our Customers

Today, we continue to build on that strong foundation with a renewed commitment to our customers. We are focused on delivering:

  • More efficient and convenient service
  • Clear, transparent pricing with competitive dispensing fees
  • A better overall patient experience

Our goal is to make your pharmacy experience easier, more affordable, and more personal.

Personalized, Community-Based Care

As an independent community pharmacy, we take the time to get to know our patients. You're not just a number here. Whether you need help managing medications, understanding your prescriptions, or navigating insurance coverage, our team is here to support you every step of the way.

We believe pharmacy care should be:

  • Accessible
  • Personal
  • Reliable

Affordable Care You Can Trust

We understand that the cost of medications matters. That's why we are committed to offering competitive, low dispensing fees, helping our patients save on every prescription without compromising on quality or care.

Proudly Serving Richmond & Steveston

Located in the heart of Steveston, we are proud to continue serving our local community with the same trusted care we've provided since 1994. We look forward to continuing to support the health and well-being of our patients for many years to come.
